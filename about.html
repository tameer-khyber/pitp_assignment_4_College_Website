About Horizon

Our history, mission, and the vibrant life on campus.

Our Heritage

A Legacy of Innovation

Founded in 1954, Khyber University began as a small college dedicated to teaching humanities and sciences. Today, we are a globally recognized research institution that empowers thousands of students each year.

Our Mission

To cultivate intellectual curiosity, ethical leadership, and a commitment to serving society through transformative education and cutting-edge research.

Our Vision

To be the world's premier university for innovative minds seeking to solve tomorrow's greatest challenges.
