Academics

Explore over 120 undergraduate and graduate degree programs.

College of Sciences

Push the Boundaries of Knowledge

The College of Sciences offers rigorous training in biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental sciences. Our students participate in groundbreaking research from their very first year.

  • B.S. / M.S. in Biology
  • B.S. in Chemistry
  • B.S. / Ph.D. in Physics

School of Engineering

Build the Future

Equipped with cutting-edge labs, our engineering programs prepare students to lead the tech industry. Specializations include computer science, mechanical engineering, and artificial intelligence.

  • B.S. in Computer Science
  • B.S. / M.S. in Mechanical Engineering
  • M.S. in Artificial Intelligence

Faculty of Arts

Inspire and Create

Rooted in a deep appreciation for culture, the Faculty of Arts offers programs in literature, history, and fine arts. Students learn to critically analyze the world and express themselves powerfully.

  • B.A. in Fine Arts
  • B.A. in History
  • B.A. in English Literature
